MANURE PLANTS


Out of 62 million tonnes of waste generated annually in India, only 27% is treated and 73% is dumped.
Seriousness about waste is essential as excessive disposal harms ecosystems, depletes resources and contributes to pollution. Responsible waste management is vital for preserving the environment/protecting wildlife and ensuring human health. Individuals must prioritize reducing, reusing and recycling to minimize the ecological footprint and foster a sustainable future.

Waste Ventures India




Waste Ventures India is a waste management social enterprise working with informal waste pickers and bulk waste generator to create pure organic manure for farmers to use.
Anybody can contribute by going to their services section and selecting organic waste processing which lets you compost your food waste either in your own premises or their facility.

Please Recycle to Save the Planet




Recycling is crucial for environmental sustainability as it conserves resources, reduces energy consumption, and minimizes waste sent to landfills.
By reusing materials, recycling lessens pollution, lowers greenhouse gas emissions, and preserves biodiversity.
It plays a key role in creating a circular economy, promoting responsible consumption, and mitigating the impact of climate change.
